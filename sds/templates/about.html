What is Silent Disco Squad?

Silent Disco Squad brings the transformational power of the dancefloor into everyday moments by organizing massively public, participant­ driven Danceparties. Using mp3 players, smartphones, and headphones, all SDS participants hear the same music at the same time, transforming everyday moments into a Silent Disco Squad.

Our Mission

Silent Disco Squad is an experimental Danceparty that creates shared music experiences in everyday moments. SDS started with there burning questions:
  • How can we carry the power of the dancefloor into everyday moments?
  • Does technology bring us closer together or further apart?
  • How can you scale a vibe without losing integrity?
We believe the Dancefloor is a powerful space deeply embedded within human culture. The Dancefloor is a transformational of expression, connection, and community. We intent to bring the power of the Dancefloor into everyday Urban moments!

How does it work?

  • 1. Join your Local Silent Disco Squad party.
  • 2. Suggest your currently - favourite superhawt dance track for the mix. The mix is crowdsourced. Choose a song that makes you groove!
  • 3. BYOD (Bring your own device): Bring your mp3 player, smartphone, walkman with the downloaded mixed uploaded and of course: your headphones!
  • On safety
    Walking as a group all wearing headphones can be dangerous, especially when crossing roads - we are a collective, watch out for your SDS brothers and sisters!
  • Ethos
    While there is an SDS organizer, the organizers create (and curates) the space. Everyone is an active participant.

Silent Disco Squad is...


Participant Driven

We all co­create each SDS by submitting Dancetracks ahead of time. The organizer picks songs and uses the selection to make the crowdsourced Dancemix. Participants bring their own headphones and music players. Each of us contributes our unique song, dance, and good vibes, creating the SDS magic~

Shared Music Experience

The Dancemix is distributed ahead of time as mp3 and become available on the website's event page. We meet up at a designated time and place then countdown "3,2,1 Play!”.  We hit play as the Dancemix starts to stream. We all listen to the same music at the same time and groove!

Massively Public

We groove outside, in public space. While there is a barrier to entry (music player + headphones), we aim to make it as easy as possible to join in an SDS.  Bring a headphone splitter and an extra pair of headphones! Everyone is invited!

Celebration

Through our dance and expression we celebrate our selves, our city, and life itself.  We respectfully spread joy and mayhem ~:D
