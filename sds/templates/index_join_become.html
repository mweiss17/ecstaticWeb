Become an SDS organizer!

Make one yourself! It's easier than you think and we will support you throughout the process.


Silent Disco Squad is a community of people aimed at turning the world into a dancefloor. Silent Disco Squad is here to empower as many people as possible to take part in and organize Silent Discos in their local community.

How to organize a SDS in 5 steps

  1. Create the event
  2. Promote your awesome SDS event
  3. Make the dancemix
    (or have an musically-inclined friend help)
  4. Have an awesome event
  5. Collect pictures and videos and show how you are
    Turning the World into a dancefloor!

Silent Disco Squad is still in alpha. If you are interested in learning more about organizing your own SDS event, please fill out the form. And we're hyper-responsive, we will talk soon!
