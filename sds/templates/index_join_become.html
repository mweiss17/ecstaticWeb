Become an SDS organizer!

Make one yourself! It's easier than you think and we will support you throughout the process.


Silent Disco Squad is a community of people aimed at Turning the World into a Dancefloor. Silent Disco Squad is here to empower as many people as possible to take part and organize Silent Disco Squads in their local community.

How to organize a SDS in 5 steps

  1. Create the event
  2. Promote your awesome SDS event
  3. Mix + Share the crowdsourced dancemix (or have and musically­-inclined friend help)
  4. Have an amazing SDS
  5. Collect pictures and videos and show how you are World into a Dancefloor!

Silent Disco Squad is still in alpha. If you are interested in learning more about organizing your own SDS event, please fill out the form.
