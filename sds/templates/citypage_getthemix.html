Participant's Guide

For a smooth danceparty


  • 1. Join your Local SDS party!
    Enter your email address to join and submit a song.
  • 2. Suggest you current/favourite superhawt dance track for the mix.
  • 3. The mix is crowdsourced.
    Choose a song that makes you groove!
  • 4. BYOD (Bring your own device)!
    Bring your mp3 player, smartphone, walkman with the downloaded mixed uploaded and of course: your headphones!

On Safety

Walking as a group all wearing headphones can be dangerous, especially when crossing roads - we are a collective, watch out for your SDS brothers and sisters!


Ethos

While there is an SDS organizer, the organizers create (and curates) the space. Everyone is an active participant.
