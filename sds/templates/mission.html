Our mission

Turning the world into a dancefloor



Silent Disco Squad is an experimental Danceparty that creates shared music experiences in everyday moments using headphones and music players to bring people together, Turning the World into a Dancefloor. SDS started with there burning questions:

  1. How can we carry the power of the dancefloor into everyday moments?
  2. Does technology bring us closer together or further apart?
  3. How can you scale a vibe without losing integrity?
We believe the Dancefloor is a powerful space deeply embedded within human culture.
The Dancefloor is a transformational of expression, connection, and community.
We intent to bring the power of the Dancefloor into everyday Urban moments!
