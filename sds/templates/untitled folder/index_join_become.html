Become an SDS organizer!

Make one yourself! It's easier than you think and we will support you throughout the process.


Silent Disco Squad is a community of people aimed at Turning the World into a Dancefloor. Silent Disco Squad is here to empower as many people as possible to take part and organize Silent Disco Squads in their local community.

How to organize a SDS in
5 steps

  1. Create the Event on SilentDiscoSquad.com and FB [Find co­hosts (media, DJ, Disco Crew)]
  2. Make the DanceMix (or have a musically­inclined friend help)
  3. Promote your awesome SDS event!
  4. Have an awesome Event!
  5. Share content here and show how you're helping turn the World into a Dancefloor
