What is SDS


Silent Disco Squad aims to bring the transformational power of the dancefloor into everyday moments by organizing massively public, participant-driven Silent Disco Squads. SDS uses mp3 players, smartphones and headphones to create a shared music experience for all participants, transforming everyday moments into a silent disco dance party.

Picture

Participant Driven


Dance track suggestions are submitted ahead of time to the SDS Organizer. The organizer picks songs and uses the selection to make a crowdsourced dance mix. Participants Bring their own Headphones and music players --> BYOD (Bring your own device)

Picture

Shared Music Experience


The Mix is distributed ahead of time as mp3 and loaded to Soundcloud. We meet up at a certain time and place and countdown "3,2,1!" We hit play as the crowdsourced dance mix starts to stream. We all listen to the same music at the same time.

Picture

Massively Public


We jam outside, in public space While there is a barrier to entry (music player + headphones), we aim to make it as easy as possible to join in an SDS. Everyone is invited!

Picture

Celebration


Through our dance and self-expression we celebrate our selves, our city, and life itself. We respectfully spread joy and mayhem ~:D
