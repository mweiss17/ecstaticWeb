Participant's Guide

For a smooth Silent Disco Experience

  • 1. Join you Local SDS party
    Enter your email address to join and submit a song.
  • 2. Suggest you currently-favourite superhawt dance track for the mix
    The mix is crowdsourced. Choose a song that makes you groove!
  • 3. BYOD (Bring your own device)
    Bring your mp3 player, smartphone, walkman with the downloaded mixed uploaded and of course: your headphones!
  • 4. On safety
    Walking as a group all wearing headphones can be dangerous, especially when crossing roads - we are a collective, watch out for your SDS brothers and sisters!
  • 5. Ethos While there is an SDS organizer, the organizers create (and curates) the space. Everyone is an active participant.
